# /recommend genres

Set your preferred genres for book recommendations. Use this command to tell Elysium what kinds of books you love—so all recommendations match your tastes and interests.

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

<h3 className="heading-with-icon">
  <img src="/img/icons/chat.svg" alt="" className="heading-icon" />
  <span>Usage</span>
</h3>
```bash
/recommend genres <genres>
```
- `<genres>` – A list of your favorite book genres (e.g., Fantasy, Romance, Mystery). You can include multiple genres separated by commas or spaces.

:::secondary
**Set your preferred genre with this command.**
:::

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

<h3 className="heading-with-icon">
  <img src="/img/icons/example.svg" alt="" className="heading-icon" />
  <span>Example</span>
</h3>
```bash
/recommend genres Fantasy, Mystery, Sci-Fi
```
This will:
- Update your genre preferences for Elysium’s book recommendation engine
- Ensure future recommendations are more likely to match your favorite genres
- Personalize the book discovery experience to your reading style

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

<h3 className="heading-with-icon">
  <img src="/img/icons/notes.svg" alt="" className="heading-icon" />
  <span>Notes</span>
</h3>
> - You can update your genres anytime by running this command again
> - The more genres you list, the broader your recommendations will be
> - For best results, use common genre names
> - Your preferences are private and only affect your recommendations

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

<h3 className="heading-with-icon">
  <img src="/img/icons/related.svg" alt="" className="heading-icon" />
  <span>Related Commands</span>
</h3>
`/recommend get` – Get an instant book recommendation

`/recommend set` – Set all your recommendation preferences at once

`/recommend show` – View your current recommendation preferences

`/recommend language` – Set your preferred language

`/recommend notify` – Choose where to receive book recs
